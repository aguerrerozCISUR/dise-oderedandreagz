Module 3: Comparing Routines (Part 1)

Comparing routines

Module 3: Collaborative Board

Objective:

Collaborate with classmates to complete tasks and share ideas on a digital board. Practice English by describing your daily routines and comparing them with others.

Instructions:

  1. Open the collaborative board below.
  2. Write a description of your daily routine in English.
  3. Compare your routine with a classmate's routine by commenting on their post.
  4. Practice using comparative phrases, such as "My routine is busier than yours" or "I wake up earlier than my classmate."
