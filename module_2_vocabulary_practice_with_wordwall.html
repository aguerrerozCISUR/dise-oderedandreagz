Module 2: Vocabulary Practice with Wordwall

Objective:

Practice and enhance your understanding of daily routines vocabulary through engaging interactive activities on Wordwall.

Tasks to Complete:

  • Access the Wordwall activity by clicking the provided link or embedded resource.
  • Complete the vocabulary exercises, including matching, word searches, and sentence-building tasks.
  • Take note of any unfamiliar words and review their meanings to expand your vocabulary.

Time Allocation:

Spend approximately 20-30 minutes on this activity to ensure you cover all exercises thoroughly.

Activities:

Activity 1: Hangman

Activity 2: Video Practice

Activity 3: Daily Routine Board Game
