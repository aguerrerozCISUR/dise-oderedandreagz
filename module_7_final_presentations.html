Module 7: Final Presentations

🎤 Final Presentations

Objective:

Show what know, present your daily routines in English.

Steps:

1. Work with your group to prepare your presentation.

2. Use visual aids like pictures or posters to make it fun.

3. Take turns presenting your routines to the class.

4. Listen to feedback and celebrate your success!

Materials:

  • Cardboard or old boxes 📦📦📦
  • Magazines or newspapers (for pictures) 📰🎴📚
  • Markers, pens, or colored pencils 🖍️✏️
  • Scissors and glue (if available) ✂️🧴
