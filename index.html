About This Course

Welcome to the English vocabulary practice course designed for A2-level students in the medical field. This course focuses on enhancing your language skills through engaging, interactive modules that revolve around daily routines and academic contexts.

What You'll Learn

  • Key vocabulary related to daily routines and academic activities.
  • Sentence-building and grammar applications for real-world contexts.
  • Interactive practice using platforms like Wordwall and Canva.

How to Navigate

Start with Module 1 and proceed sequentially. Each module includes specific activities, practice exercises, and evaluations. Click on the links in the navigation bar above to access the content.

Developed by Teacher Andrea Guerrero Zuluaga for the subject "Diseño y construcción de RED para la Especialización en TIC para la educación".
